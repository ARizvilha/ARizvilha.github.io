Home and School Association

LET'S MAKE A DIFFERENCE TOGETHER

LHA Home & School is a not-for-profit association, comprised of a group of dedicated parent volunteers working in collaboration
with the school’s administrative body and its teachers towards one common goal: Enhancing our children’s educational
experience and learning environment! We are always looking for volunteers and helping hands.

What does Home & School Do?

Home & School holds fundraisers such as; Bake Sales, Jog-a-thon, Free Dress Day, Raffle and TCBY. With monies raised, the
Home and School Association purchases sports equipment and donates funds for student’s events such as the Back to School bash and Scholarships to name a few. We are always looking for volunteers and helping hands. Our committee meets once a month at the LHA Junior campus.

How You Can Help

  1. -Attend our events
  2. -Support our drives and sales
  3. -Make a monetary donation (Tax receipt)
  4. -Donate items/tickets that we can raffle and sell
  5. -Donate your time and possibly become a member
