Certificate of Eligibility

In order to qualify for English Instruction in Quebec Public Schools, students must meet certain eligibility criteria under the Charter of the French Language as set out by the Quebec government. For more information on the Charter of the French Language, or to apply for a certificate of eligibility, please visit the registration information section of the EMSB Website at: www.emsb.qc.ca All applications for the Certificate of Eligibility for Secondary students must be done at the English Montreal School Board Administration Building.
